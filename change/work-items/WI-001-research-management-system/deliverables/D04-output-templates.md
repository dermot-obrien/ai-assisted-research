# Deliverable: WI-001-D04 Output Templates

> Activity: WI-001-A4 Output Templates (ARXIV & Blog)
> Status: Complete

## Overview

This deliverable provides the Markdown templates for the two primary research outputs: a narrative-style blog post and a formal ARXIV-style academic paper.

## 1. Blog-style Narrative Template (`templates/blog_template.md`)

A storytelling-focused template designed to share the research journey, the "why" behind a specific hypothesis, and a narrative summary of the results.

### Structure
- **The Hook**: Why this research avenue was chosen.
- **The Search**: A narrative description of the solution space exploration.
- **The Result**: A high-level summary of performance gains (or losses).
- **The Next Frontier**: What this means for the next node in the DAG.

## 2. ARXIV-style Formal Paper Template (`templates/arxiv_template.md`)

A rigorous, academic template designed for formal submission and detailed documentation of the research methodology and data.

### Structure
- **Abstract**: A concise summary of the research and findings.
- **Introduction**: Problem definition and SOTA baseline.
- **Methodology**: Technical details of the variant and the benchmarking process.
- **Results**: Detailed performance data and statistical analysis.
- **Conclusion**: Final assessment of the hypothesis node and its impact on the project objective.
